Why Horizon?

Three Pillars of Strength

Built for couples and parent-child relationships alike. One policy, two lives protected, and a legacy for the next generation.

🛡️
Dual-Life Protection

A single policy covers two people at once. If either or both pass away before retirement, the benefit is paid immediately to the survivor or heirs.

💰
Lifetime Retirement Income

From age 60, you receive annuity payments for a full 20 years — whether together or as a surviving partner after your loved one has passed.

🌿
Generational Legacy

A 10-year guarantee ensures payments continue to your children or heirs — even if both policyholders pass before Year 10 of retirement.
